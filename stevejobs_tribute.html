Steve Jobs Timeline

  • 1955 - Born in San francisco and adopted
  • 1960 - Moved to silicone Valley
  • 1968 - 13-year-old Steve Jobs calls up Bill Hewlett and gets a summer job at the HP factory.
  • 1973 - Steve spends the fall semester at Reed College, Oregon, then drops out.
  • 1974 - Steve gets his first job at video game maker Atari, and later makes a trip to India to 'seek enlightenment' with his college friend Dan Kottke
  • 1977 - Apple makes a huge sensation at the West Coast Computer Faire with a prototype Apple II.
  • 24 Jan 1984 - Macintosh is launched in great fanfare at Apple's annual shareholder meeting.
  • 1985 - Steve Jobs resigns from Apple and starts NeXT with five other refugees from Apple. Apple announces it will sue NeXT.
  • 1986 - Jobs buys the computer division of George Lucas' ILM for $10 million and incorporates it as Pixar.
  • 1996 - Apple, which was desperately looking for a modern operating system to buy, eventually buys NeXT for $400 million.
  • 1997 - Steve Jobs is named interim CEO in his place and installs his NeXT executive team at the top of Apple.
  • 1998 - Steve Jobs introduces Apple's revolutionary iMac at the Flint Center auditorium in Cupertino
  • 2000 - Steve Jobs becomes Apple CEO
  • 2001 - fter an 8-month crash development program, Steve Jobs unveils iPod
  • 2007 - Steve Jobs introduces iPhone and its revolutionary touch-screen interface.
  • 2008 Steve Jobs introduces MacBook Air
  • 2011Steve Jobs dies at home surrounded by family
